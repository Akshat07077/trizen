Trizen Packaging manufactures tamper-proof packaging from our GMP-compliant, ISO 9001:2015 certified facility in Vapi, Gujarat — custom thermoformed tamper-proof and tamper-evident packaging components for pharmaceutical products. Tamper-proof packaging manufacturer from Vapi for pharmaceutical companies, OTC drug manufacturers, and pharma exporters. As a tamper-proof packaging manufacturer from India, we supply thermoformed tamper-evident pharmaceutical packaging for domestic and export markets.

Pharmaceutical tamper-proof packaging communicates pharmaceutical product integrity at the retail and dispensing point — any physical evidence of tamper indicates the drug product may have been adulterated. Thermoformed tamper-proof packaging components include tamper-evident blister base components (heat-sealed to foil with a seal that shows visible damage if opened and resealed), and tamper-evident container inserts. A pharmaceutical tamper proof container design must show permanent, visible damage when tampered with.

⚠️ Trizen Packaging Scope Note: Trizen manufactures the thermoformed packaging component — the blister base tray, vial tray, syringe tray, diagnostic kit tray, or strip/sachet packaging. Drug filling into blisters, foil sealing (lidding), batch printing, and serialisation are performed by the pharmaceutical manufacturer at their GMP-validated facility. All packaging components produced in our Cleanroom Class 8 facility under GMP and ISO 9001:2015.
